Pan Roasted Brussels Sprouts with Bacon



These simply prepared Pan Roasted Brussels Sprouts are cooked al dente, so they still have a nice bite.
They're generously topped with crispy bacon for an added crunch.


Ingredients

Makes 4 to 6 servings

  • 4 strips thick-cut bacon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pound Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1/2 large onion, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Cook bacon in a large skillet over medium-high heat until crispy. Remove to a paper towel-lined plate, then roughly chop.

  2. In same pan with bacon fat, melt butter over high heat.

  3. Add onions and Brussels Sprouts and cook, stirring occasionally, until sprouts are golden brown, 8 to 10 minutes.

  4. Season with salt and pepper, to taste, and toss bacon back into pan. Serve immediately.
