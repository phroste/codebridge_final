About

Hi, I'm Steven. Growing up, I was privileged to be raised by the best chef in the world - my Mom. This website is dedicated to her for introducing me to one of my favorite passions in the world - cooking. I truly believe that sharing a simple meal is one of the best ways to show someone you care. I hope my humble website shows you how easy it is to prepare a satisfying meal and inspires you to create something delicious. Thanks for visiting!

I am excited to announce that my book, "Family Recipes from the World's Best Chef (Mom)" is currently in pre-production and is scheduled to be released in December 2018!
Thank you all for your support!
